GeoSolve is an advanced, AI-powered Math & Geometry Web Application developed by CALCORE.
The goal is to create a smooth, modern, fast web app that can:
solve math problems, explain steps, analyze images, perform OCR, visualize geometry, and provide AI tutoring.

GeoSolve should function as:

1. Math Problem Solver

Accepts math queries like:

integrate x^2 sin(x)

Supports algebra, calculus, trigonometry, matrices, and statistics.
